# Body

In this repository, the `SKILL.md` for a skillset SHALL contain a minimal Markdown body.

## Canonical body format

- The body SHALL contain a single H1: `INSTRUCTIONS`.
- The body SHALL explicitly direct the reader to the mandatory pipeline index: `.pipelines/.INDEX.md`.
- No other prose or sections are permitted.

## Canonical example

```md
# INSTRUCTIONS

1. Refer to `.pipelines/.INDEX.md`.
```
